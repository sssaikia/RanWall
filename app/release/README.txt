Description
=============================
Ranwall is an application designed to automatically change the wallpaper of your device.

Open the application and your all set, forget about it. The app will surprise you with a new and beautifull wallpaper each time you unlock your device.





How to install
=============================
Download the apk and install it.




Also
=============================
If you don't like the notification, try disabling it. But sometimes your system kills the background services in order to maintain RAM sufficiency. So the app might crash unexpectedly. 




Recent added feature (v1.01)
=============================
Download your fav wallpaper in normal or high quality.

find the details about the picture.
